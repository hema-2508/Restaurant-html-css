About Spice Garden

Learn about our family, our food, and our love for Indian cooking

Our Story

Spice Garden started in 2009 when the our family came to Nagaland from Tirunelveli, India. We missed the taste of home-cooked Indian food, so we decided to open our own restaurant.

We wanted to share the real flavors of India with everyone. Today, we are happy to see families from all backgrounds enjoying our food and coming back again and again.

Meet Our Team

Our kitchen team knows Indian cooking very well. They learned from their mothers and grandmothers in India. Every dish is made with care and love.

Amit Singh

What Makes Us Special

We believe good food brings people together. Here's what makes Spice Garden different:

Fresh Spices from India

We get our spices directly from India every few months. This keeps our food tasting fresh and real, just like in Indian homes.

Family Recipes

All our recipes come from our family in India. We cook the same way our grandmothers did, with traditional methods and lots of love.

Friendly Service

We treat every customer like family. If you don't know what to order, we're happy to help you choose something you'll love.

Clean and Safe

Our kitchen is always clean and we follow all safety rules. We want you to enjoy your meal without any worries.

Our Restaurant

Spice Garden has a warm and cozy feeling with Indian decorations and soft music. We have comfortable seating for families, couples, and groups of friends.

Whether you want a quiet dinner or a celebration with many people, we have space for you. Come visit us and feel the warmth of Indian hospitality!
